Video Analysis Overview

On court, everything feels different; faster, more intense, more instinctive. But what if you could step outside that moment and see your game with fresh eyes?

My Squash Video Analysis Service helps you do exactly that. By breaking down your movement, shot choices, and patterns of play, I highlight details that might otherwise go unnoticed.

Small adjustments can have a big impact, and understanding your own habits is the first step to making meaningful improvements.

Whether you’re working on improving your match tactics or trying to hitting better shots, a clearer perspective can make all the difference.

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are the most commonly asked questions. If you have others please feel free to contact me and I will do my best to answer them.

After using this service, will I be able to beat my arch-nemesis?
ABSOLUTELY! Well, possibly. If your video is of you playing him or her, make sure you tell me that you specifically want to beat this player and I will give you tips to not only improve your game, but also counter your nemesis’ strengths and exploit their weaknesses.

Do I need permission from my opponent to record my match?
I don't require proof that permission has been granted, but I suggest you at least ask your opponent before you record and tell them that it will be used for an analysis. I have never heard of an opponent refusing, although it might happen.

English is not my first language, can I still use the service?
Yes, definitely. If I know English is not your first language, I will speak slower, use clearer words and avoid phrasal verbs. Please let me know when you send your initial email.

What happens if I don’t feel I received good value for money?
Notice that you do not pay until AFTER you have received the feedback video. I ask you to pay AFTER because I understand this is a new way to do this. I trust you and I hope you trust me. If you really do not feel that what you got was good value, then you can pay what you think it is worth.

What type of feedback will I receive?
I focus on two aspects: the tactical and technical. I will highlight tactical mistakes you made, but also highlight the correct shot selection too. I will talk about the technical issues you may have and how that affects your tactical choices.

I am a junior, can I still use this service?
Yes, but I require contact and communication through a parent or guardian.

Can I pay with my credit card?
Yes, but I require all payments to go through Stripe. You can use your credit card to pay via Stripe.

I only have a mobile phone. Can I use that to record my video?
I have received videos recorded via a mobile phone and it was fine. of course, it depends on the quality of your phone and where it is positioned.

Can I see a sample feedback video?
Yes, of course. Please scroll down for the video.

Assessment Questions

These are the questions you answer to receive your Personalised Practice Plan (PPP)

General Background

➤  How long have you been playing squash?

➤  What’s your current skill level (beginner, intermediate, advanced, competitive)?

➤  Do you have any formal coaching experience or are mostly self-taught?

➤  Are you right or left handed?

➤  Briefly describe your usual squash week, i.e. how often and the types of training you do, what kind of matches you play: team, tournament, league etc.

➤  Do you have a training partner of similar standard?

➤  How often do you currently perform: solo drills, pair’s drills, condition games, practice matches, serious matches, ghosting, coaching sessions and gym/fitness sessions?

➤  Do you have any injuries or limitations I should consider when designing your plan?

Goals & Motivation

➤  What are your top 1-2 squash goals right now? (e.g., better consistency, winning more matches, improving a specific shot)

➤  Are you preparing for a tournament, personal challenge, or general skill enhancement?

➤  What is your primary motivation for seeking a personalised practice plan?

➤  Are there specific match situations you want to improve? (e.g., Finishing games, handling pressure)

➤  Are there any drills or exercises you would like me to omit from the plan?

Technical Self-Assessment

➤  How would you describe your style of play?

➤  Which 3 shots or skills do you feel most confident in?

➤  Which 3 areas frustrate you the most or feel like weaknesses?

➤  How would you rate your court movement and positioning?

➤  What specific aspects of your game are you looking to improve?

➤  Do you struggle more with technical execution (e.g., poor drops, weak volleys) or tactical decisions (e.g., shot selection, positioning)?

➤  What aspects of match play do you find most challenging?

➤  Are there specific professional players whose style you admire or wish to emulate?

In addition to the questions above, I may contact you to clarify something or ask something not already asked.

Your Quick Guide to Getting Started

What is Discord?

Discord is a free communication platform that allows communities to connect through text, voice, and video channels. Our BetterSquash server is a virtual space for squash enthusiasts to learn, share, and grow together.

Server Overview

Think of a Discord server like a multi-room building:

  • Public channels are rooms open to everyone
  • Private channels are rooms exclusive to service purchasers

Public Channels

The public channels include:

  • General Squash Chat
  • Solo Routines
  • Video Analysis
  • Challenges
  • Personalised Practice Plans
  • Referrals
  • Squash Tournaments (Spoiler Alert: Contains match results!)

Once you have access to one public channel, you have access to all of them.

Private Channels

Exclusive channels are available to users who have purchased specific services. When you buy a service, you'll receive:

  • A unique referral code
  • Links to your private channels
  • A welcome email with access details

How to Join

  1. Create a Discord account
  2. Use the browser version or download the app
  3. Find invitation links on the BetterSquash website or service PDFs
  4. When joining private channels, have your unique referral code ready. You will need to send me a direct message (DM) to share that code.

Community Purpose

The BetterSquash Discord isn't just about communication, it's about collective improvement. By sharing experiences, suggesting drill modifications, and engaging with each other, we all become better squash players.

Moderation Note: I expect everybody to remain polite and friendly at all times.
