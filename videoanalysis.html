Video Analysis Overview

On court, everything feels different; faster, more intense, more instinctive. But what if you could step outside that moment and see your game with fresh eyes?

My Squash Video Analysis Service helps you do exactly that. By breaking down your movement, shot selection, and patterns of play, I highlight details that might otherwise go unnoticed.

Small adjustments can have a big impact, and understanding your own habits is the first step to making meaningful improvements.

Whether you’re working on improving your match tactics or trying to hitting better shots, a clearer perspective can make all the difference.

Send me your footage, and let’s take a closer look.

How It Works

It's Easy Peasy, Lemon Squeezy

➤  You send me a recording of a competitive match and I do a "live recording analysis", which normally last about 20 minutes. The best type of matches to send me are the very close ones or the ones you lose. I can perform the analysis on friendly matches, but the opponent must be of equal standard.

➤  Use a transfer service like WeTransfer to send me the video or link to an online video. If the video is longer than 8 minutes, let me know the start time you want me to analyse from.

➤  During the analysis recording I talk about tactical and technical aspects of your game, pausing the video to highlight my points.

➤  I post the video on my YouTube channel and make it unlisted, so only you can find and see it.

➤  When you are ready to start or if you have any questions, email me. I will reply and give you an estimated delivery date.

Your Quick Guide to Getting Started

What is Discord?

Discord is a free communication platform that allows communities to connect through text, voice, and video channels. Our BetterSquash server is a virtual space for squash enthusiasts to learn, share, and grow together.

Server Overview

Think of a Discord server like a multi-room building:

  • Public channels are rooms open to everyone
  • Private channels are rooms exclusive to service purchasers

Public Channels

The public channels include:

  • General Squash Chat
  • Solo Routines
  • Video Analysis
  • Challenges
  • Personalised Practice Plans
  • Referrals
  • Squash Tournaments (Spoiler Alert: Contains match results!)

Once you have access to one public channel, you have access to all of them.

Private Channels

Exclusive channels are available to users who have purchased specific services. When you buy a service, you'll receive:

  • A unique referral code
  • Links to your private channels
  • A welcome email with access details

How to Join

  1. Create a Discord account
  2. Use the browser version or download the app
  3. Find invitation links on the BetterSquash website or service PDFs
  4. When joining private channels, have your unique referral code ready. You will need to send me a direct message (DM) to share that code.

Community Purpose

The BetterSquash Discord isn't just about communication, it's about collective improvement. By sharing experiences, suggesting drill modifications, and engaging with each other, we all become better squash players.

Moderation Note: I expect everybody to remain polite and friendly at all times.
