This Is The Process

It's Easy Peasy, Lemon Squeezy

1. You choose your analysis option: Short-Form or Long-Form

2. You send me a competitive match recording via a transfer service like WeTransfer or a link to an online video.

3. I analyse your video in real-time, explaining my thoughts as I see the footage for the first time, pausing to discuss tactical and technical aspects.

4. I send you an email with the link to your private video analysis and your Summary PDF.

5. You watch the video and read the Summary PDF.

6. You contact me if you have any questions.

7. You follow my advice and perform the assigned training.

8. You and your opponents notice your improvement!
