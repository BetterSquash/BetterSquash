Challenges Overview

Each month, receive a PDF detailing 6 challenges for you to attempt in the following areas: Solo drills, Pairs’ drills, Condition Games, Matchplay, Mindset and Physical.

Each challenge has a specific and clearly defined purpose, which is explained on the PDF sheet.

There are two tiers available: Core where you only receive the PDF sheet and Complete; where you receive the PDF, plus links to explainer videos and access to Discord channels where you will be joining a community of players who are all pushing themselves to be better - just like YOU!

Every month, you’ll tackle new challenges alongside others who share your desire for improvement.

Curious how it works? Download one or both of the free sample PDF sheets and try it for yourself. The samples include explainer videos and limited access to the Discord server channels.

Frequently Asked Questions

What’s included in the price?

You’ll receive 12 groups of challenges (4 challenges per group) delivered monthly, each targeting a specific area of squash.

Is this suitable for beginners?

The challenges are designed for intermediate and advanced players, but motivated beginners can also benefit. I suggest you try the sample and see how you feel about it.

Do I have to do all the challenges in each group?

No, you can choose which challenges you like.

Do I have to watch the Explainer Video?

No, that's an optional extra. The full details are in the PDF sheet, but the video may go into more detail about how to perform the challenge and why the challenge is beneficial.

Do I have to join the Discord server?

No, I created it for people who like to chat about the challenges with other users and to discuss their progress etc.

Can I share the PPDF sheet with my friends?

With a couple of friends, yes, but if you have a training squad or group, then please purchase the Commercial Licence.
This will allow you to post the PDF on your facility's noticeboard and make a reason number of photocopies.
However, only up to 10 users can join the Discord server with a commercial licence.

I have a question not listed here, how can I contact you?

Send an email to phillip@bettersquash.com or join the Discord server and ask in there.
