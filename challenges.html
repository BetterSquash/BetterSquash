Frequently Asked Questions

Got some questions? Try here.

What’s included in the price?
One Set includes 12 groups of challenges (6 challenges per set), delivered monthly if in the current set.

Is this suitable for beginners?
The challenges are designed for intermediate and advanced players, but motivated beginners can also benefit. I recommend trying the free samples to see if it suits you.

Do I have to complete all the challenges in each group?
No, you can pick and choose the challenges that interest you.

Do I need to watch the Explainer Video?
No, it's optional. The PDF includes all the necessary details, but the video may provide additional insights into how to perform the challenge and why it is beneficial.

Do I have to join the BetterSquash Discord server?
No, it's entirely optional. The server is there for players who want to discuss the challenges, share progress, and ask questions.

Can I share the PDF with my friends?
You can share it with a couple of friends, but if you have a training squad or group, please purchase the Commercial Licence.
This allows you to post the PDF on your facility's noticeboard and make a reasonable number of photocopies.

Do I need special equipment to complete the challenges?
No, most challenges can be done with standard squash equipment. Some solo, pairs' drills and condition games may require targets, but a sheet of A4/Letter sized paper will be perfect.

Can I access previous months' challenges if I join later in the year?
Yes! When you sign up, you get access to all challenges released so far in the current set.

I have a question not listed here. How can I contact you?
Send an email to challenges@BetterSquash.com or join the BetterSquash Discord server and ask there.
