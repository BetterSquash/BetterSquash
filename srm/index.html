What Is It?

A monthly 30-minute (more or less) solo routine for club players. Each routine focuses on different aspects of hitting, technique, swing, accuracy, power and control.

Why You Need It

Most players need guidance and objectives when they do solo drilling. They know some drills, but they don't have a training plan. Having a plan, and sticking to it BTW, can make the difference between improving and using your time effectively, and wasting your time and not improving.

What You Get

Every month, I send you an email that contains a link to the PDF Solo Routine sheet - for you to print or save to your mobile device, and a link to a brief explainer video (under 5 minutes). The video does NOT have any voice, so you can easily watch at any time. The email contains links to all the previous months' PDF sheets and videos, making it easy to use a different routine if the desire strikes you.

It also contains my Solo Drill Guidelines, to ensure you maximise your time on court, as well as a few reminders.

How Much Does It Cost?

The investment, yes I do think that's the right term, is 25 Euros for the full calendar year. It doesn't matter when you join, you get the full year's worth of routines. So, even if you joining in September for example, the first email you get links to ALL the previous routines.

The 25 Euros is for individuals only. If you run a training group or another organisation, including clubs, the investment is 50 Euros per calendar year. There's no way for me to know, so I will have to trust you. For that 50 Euros, you are allowed to make photocopies (within reason - and NEVER sell them) and/or pin the sheet onto a notice board.
