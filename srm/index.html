What Is It?

A monthly 30-minute (more or less) solo routine for club players. Each routine focuses on different aspects of hitting, technique, swing, accuracy, power and control.

Why You Need It

Most players need guidance and objectives when they do solo drilling. They know some drills, but they don't have a training plan. Having a plan, and sticking to it!, can make the difference between improving and using your time effectively, and wasting your time and not improving.

What You Get

Every month, I send you an email that contains a link to the PDF Solo Routine sheet and a link to a brief explainer video (under 5 minutes). The video does NOT have any voiceover, so you can easily watch at any time. The PDf can be printed or simply saved to your mobile device.

The email also contains links to all the previous months' PDF sheets and videos, making it easy to use a different routine if you want to. In fact, it's a good idea to switch around routines, once you have performed it a few times.

The email also contains my Solo Drill Guidelines, to ensure you maximise your time on court, as well as a few reminders.

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are the questions I have most frequently been asked. If your question is not here, please feel free to contact me and I will do my best to answer you.

How often do I need to perform the routine? I recommend at least once a week, but if you can do more without sacrificing your pairs drills//conditioned games and matches, then do them more often.

What if I find some of the drills too difficult? Firstly, don't worry. If everything was easy there would be little point doing it. Do your best to follow the instructions for the drill and keep trying. If needed, try a bouncier ball, i.e. from double yellow to single yellow dot.

What does "club" player mean? It can be difficult to exactly define a players level unless they play competitions, and even then each players has different strengths and weaknesses. By club player, I mean a person who players regularly, probably even in some competitions and has the ability to hit most shots - at least in practice.
