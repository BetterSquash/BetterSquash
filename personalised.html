Personalised Practice Plans Overview

In squash, improvement isn’t just about playing more - it’s about practicing with purpose.

Many players struggle to structure their training efficiently, often unsure which drills will yield real progress.

My Personalised Practicing Plans (PPP) provides a clear, systematic approach to practice, tailored to your individual needs.

Each PPP comes in two parts:
» Solo Plan – A focused series of solo drills designed to refine technical skills and ball control.
» Paired Plan – A combination of pairs' drills, conditioned games, and match play rules to develop tactical awareness, shot selection, and competitive adaptability.

Rather than generic training advice, these plans are built around your specific strengths, weaknesses and objectives, thereby ensuring each session has a clear purpose.

By training with precision, you can maximize your court time and accelerate your development.

Frequently Asked Questions

Got some questions? Try here.

General

What are Personalised Practice Plans?
Personalised Practice Plans (PPP) are custom-designed practice programmes tailored specifically to player's individual skills, objectives and commitment. Based on answers to approximately 20 questions, a PPP is created to help players achieve their maximum potential.

How is a Personalised Practice Plan different from generic training?
Unlike one-size-fits-all approaches, these plans are specifically crafted to each player's unique situation. The difference is similar to buying clothes "off the rack" or having them tailormade for you.

Getting Started

How do I get my first Personalised Practice Plan?
The process is simple: 1: Purchase the plan type of your choice. 2: Respond to the PPP assessment questions. 3. Wait about a week (a little more if you have selected the video option) 4. Receive and read your custom-designed practice plan. 5. Begin your personalised training journey.

What information do I need to provide in the assessment?
You can see the complete list of questions in the Assessment Questions section below.

Do you offer quantity discounts? For example, if I purchase 6 plans.
Yes, I would offer you a 10% discount. The plans don't even need to be for the same person! Please contact me directly to discuss the details.

Do you create plans for different skill levels?
Yes, plans can be created for any skill level; from beginner to advanced player.

I have a question not answered here, what should I do?
Send an email to ppp@bettersquash.com or ask in the BetterSquash Discord Server

Using a plan

How often are the plans updated?
Plans can be modified up to one month after receiving them. After that, you will need to purchase a new one.

How do I use the plan?
Once you understand all the drills and exercises in your plan, you simply perform those drills and exercises.

How often do I have to perform the drills?
You perform the solo routine and other exercises between 2 and 3 times per week.

Do I have to perform all the drills?
No, if you find some drills too difficult or just don't want to do certain drills or exercises, then that is fine.

Do I need any specific equipment to use a PPP?
No, all the drills and exercises in your PP are performed on a squash court with a racket and balls.

I currently use your Solo Routines, would I benefit from having a PPP?
Yes, probably, but it depends on your commitment level. There's no reason why you can't use your PPP in conjunctions with the routines in the Solo Routines programme.

Can I modify my plan if my objectives change?
Yes and No. Minor changes can be made within one month of receiving your plan, but if you change your objectives, a new plan should be purchased.

Assessment Questions

These are the questions you answer to receive your Personalised Practice Plan (PPP)

General Background

➤  How long have you been playing squash?

➤  What’s your current skill level (beginner, intermediate, advanced, competitive)?

➤  Do you have any formal coaching experience or are mostly self-taught?

➤  Are you right or left handed?

➤  Briefly describe your usual squash week, i.e. how often and the types of training you do, what kind of matches you play: team, tournament, league etc.

➤  Do you have a training partner of similar standard?

➤  How often do you currently perform: solo drills, pair’s drills, condition games, practice matches, serious matches, ghosting, coaching sessions and gym/fitness sessions?

➤  Do you have any injuries or limitations I should consider when designing your plan?

Goals & Motivation

➤  What are your top 1-2 squash goals right now? (e.g., better consistency, winning more matches, improving a specific shot)

➤  Are you preparing for a tournament, personal challenge, or general skill enhancement?

➤  What is your primary motivation for seeking a personalised practice plan?

➤  Are there specific match situations you want to improve? (e.g., Finishing games, handling pressure)

➤  Are there any drills or exercises you would like me to omit from the plan?

Technical Self-Assessment

➤  How would you describe your style of play?

➤  Which 3 shots or skills do you feel most confident in?

➤  Which 3 areas frustrate you the most or feel like weaknesses?

➤  How would you rate your court movement and positioning?

➤  What specific aspects of your game are you looking to improve?

➤  Do you struggle more with technical execution (e.g., poor drops, weak volleys) or tactical decisions (e.g., shot selection, positioning)?

➤  What aspects of match play do you find most challenging?

➤  Are there specific professional players whose style you admire or wish to emulate?

In addition to the questions above, I may contact you to clarify something or ask something not already asked.
