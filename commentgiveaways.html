Free Comment Giveaway Overview

At the beginning of each month, I select the best comment made on my YouTube channel the previous month.

The winner selects either a free set of Solo Routines or a free set of Challenges.

I define "best" as being a comment that raises an interesting point or asks a really good question.

The comment doesn't have to be on a recent video.

How The Comment Giveaway Works

Let me explain the process

When I select a winning comment, I will reply to that comment saying the commenter has won a monthly comment. giveaway and include how to contact me. I use the "@username" YouTube function to ensure that the commenter gets notified that a reply has been made on one of their comments.

The winner emails me and I reply to them telling them to edit their winning comment with a keyword which I give them. Once I see keyword in the winning comment, I send their prize to the email address they used. (I do this to stop just anybody emailing me saying they made the comment.)

I will pin that comment to the top of the comment thread on the video they mae the comment on.

In addition, I will make a community post on YouTube announcing the winner, as well as announcing the details at the end of my next video.

One Step at a Time.

The Rules Of The Comment Giveaway

Please don't find any loopholes - if you do, please let me know!
  • Once I announce the winner, they have three months to respond to my initial announcement. I know that not everybody logins to check their account everyday like I do, so I give them plenty of time!
  • If I don't find any great comments from the previous month, I may look back over all of the comments since the channel started and select another great comment.
  • A commenter can only win a maximum of twice per year. If a commenter is consistently posting great comments, I may offer subsequent prizes.
  • If I believe a commenter is spamming my comments system, I will delete their comments AND ban them from the channel.
  • If a commenter posts the same comment on multiple videos, irrespective of whether it is a great comment or not, that commenter will be ineligible to win for that month.
  • If I have a strong suspicion that the comment was written by AI, that comment and commenter won't be eligible to win the prize for that month. (I honestly don't believe anybody would do that, but I need to add the rule, just in case).
  • Comments like "OMG Phillip, this is the best squash video I have ever seen!" or "Gosh, you are so smart and handsome" will definitely NOT win. I'm not looking for praise but insightful questions or thoughtful comments.

Your Quick Guide to Getting Started

Discord is a free platform for communities to connect via text. The BetterSquash server is a hub for squash enthusiasts to learn, share, and improve together.

The BetterSquash Discord isn’t just for chat, it’s about collective growth. By sharing experiences, refining drills, and engaging with others, we all become better players.
