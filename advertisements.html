Advertisements

This page explains my use of advertisements on the BetterSquash website and how it supports my free articles and videos about squash

Translate↗ / Listen↗ / Share

In December 2022, I started to show advertisements on articles. I prefer the idea of an ad-free website, but having one or two advertisements per page seems a reasonable compromise.

I don't write the articles or make the videos to generate income, but any income I get, allows me to cover my costs and invest in better video equipment.

Special pages will remain ad-free.

Displaying the advertisements is a running trial and if after a year I feel the amount raised is not worth the interruption in reading flow, then I will remove the ads completely from all pages.

You can support my content creation by donating via the Buy Me A Coffee link in the footer.
