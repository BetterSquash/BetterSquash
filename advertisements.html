Advertisements

This page explains my use of advertisements on the BetterSquash website and how it supports my free articles and videos about squash

Translate↗ / Listen↗ / Share

In December 2022, I started to show advertisements on articles over one month old. This means that if you visit the website regularly and read the latest articles you won't see any advertisements, but if you visit any articles odler than one month at the time of your visit, you will see one advertisement per article.

I don't write the articles or make the videos to generate income, but any income I get, allows me to invest in better equipment, in the case of articles a new laptop to allow me to write anywhere and in the case of videos a new camera to make recording quality higher, as well as another camera equipment.

From an income perspective it is better to show ads on the new pages as they get more views, but I feel that allowing readers an ad-free experience is better in the long-term for everybody.

It doesn't seem unreasonable to me to display one advertisement per article on the odler articles. Hopefully you agree.

Final Thoughts

Displaying the advertisements is a running trial and if after a year I feel the amount raised is simply too low, then I will remove the ads from all pages.
