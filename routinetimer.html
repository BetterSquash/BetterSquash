Routine Timer Overview

Improving your ball control requires structured practice, and that's exactly what the BetterSquash Solo Routines programme delivers.

The programme includes 12 carefully designed routines, each supplied in PDF format with a corresponding explainer video.

Upon signing up, you'll receive a unique three-digit code for each of the 12 routines.

The Routine Timer is a companion app that lets you practice without needing to refer to the PDF.

When you're ready to practice, simply choose a routine and enter its code into the app's start page.

The app displays each drill with timing information and provides both on-screen and audio instructions to guide you through the routine.

You can easily repeat drills or skip ahead to customize your practice session.

For maximum convenience, follow the instructions and guidance using your mobile device's speakers or by wearing earphones during your training.

The best way to understand Routine Timer's benefits is to use it. Try it now with the sample Solo Routine.

Start improving your ball control today by signing up for the Solo Routines programme and take your practice to the next level with the Routine Timer!
