Solo Routines Overview

Improving your ball control requires structured practice, and that's exactly what the BetterSquash Solo Routines programme delivers.

Each set in the programme includes 12 carefully designed routines, each supplied in PDF format with a corresponding explainer video.

Use the Routine Timer companion app to receive visual and audio guidance during the routines.
