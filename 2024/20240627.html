Watch Squash For Free Online

There are plenty of places to watch professionals squash online for free, and here are the ones I know about.

27 June 2024 / 1-Min Read / Translate

Introduction

Just to be clear, this article is NOT about getting access to the PSA Squash TV content for free. If you can't go in person, watching live online is the next best thing, and after that, watching recorded matches. The PSA World Tour TV AKA Squash TV is fantastic, but not everybody can afford to pay the subscription. So I created a Reddit thread asking for websites, channels, services that showed free high-level squash. This article displays the suggestions, plus others I found.

I believe that some sort of notification service that people can sign up to to hear about broadcast schedules would be popular, but for now you will just have to subscribe to the channels and hope you get notified.
