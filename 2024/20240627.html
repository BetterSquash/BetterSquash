Watch Squash For Free Online

There are plenty of places to watch professionals squash online for free, and here are the ones I know about. But just to be clear, this article is NOT about getting access to the PSA Squash TV content for free.

27 June 2024 / 1-Min Read / Translate

Introduction

If you can't go in person, watching live online is the next best thing, and after that, watching recorded matches. The PSA World Tour TV AKA Squash TV is fantastic, but not everybody can afford to pay the subscription. So I created a Reddit thread asking for websites, channels, services that showed free high-level squash. This article displays the suggestions, plus others I found.

I believe that some sort of notification service that people can sign up to to hear about broadcast schedules would be popular, but for now you will just have to subscribe to the channels and hope you get notified.

If you know of other websites, channels or matches that should be added to this page, please contact me and I will add them - thank you.

Channels

  • Pro Squash Challenge YouTube Channel. A series of top level pro squash challenge matches providing vital competition and financial remuneration for professional players during the Covid-19 pandemic. All matches are played according to government regulations. Squash loving individuals and companies sponsor the games and it is hoped that the series will grow.
  • PSA Challenger Tour YouTube Channel. A collection of videos and live streams from the Professional Squash Association Challenger Tour. The livestreams are run by the tournaments themselves and as such the PSA and PSA SquashTV have no control over the contents or quality of streaming.
  • Squash Fi YouTube Channel. All Finnish squash matches recorded, will be added here. All major tournaments are streamed Live here.
  • Squash Australia YouTube Channel. Squash Australia's YouTube channel hosts our own video clips but also playlists including some of our great squash players and rising stars of Australian Squash and major tournaments across the country.
  • Federsquash Figs YouTube Channel. The Videos of the Italian Squash Federation.
  • Dutch Squash Federation YouTube Channel. A channel with a few matches from The Netherlands.
  • Squash Japan YouTube Channel. This is the official channel of the Japan Squash Association. If you are interested in squash, please visit the official website.
  • Hong Kong Squash YouTube Channel. A channel with a some senior & junior matches from Hong Kong.
  • India Squash YouTube Channel. A channel with plenty of matches as well as coaching advice. See also India Squash TV.
  • US Squash YouTube Channel. Plenty of videos from US Squash, including coaching, refereeing and of course real matches.
  • Legends of Squash 2016 YouTube Channel. Matches from the Bermuda Legends of Squash 2016.
  • Bermuda Squash YouTube Channel. Matches from the Legends of Squash 2019, in Bermuda.
  • Canada Cup Squash YouTube Channel. A few videos from the Canada Cup 2020, the final features Paul Coll and Tarek Momen.
  • Squash Site YouTube Channel. Plenty of matches and also lots of "breakfast with Fram" videos.
  • Squash XL YouTube Channel. Professional Squash Team - training, playing, running events on our journey to climb the world rankings
  • Squash Stream YouTube Channel. A Canadian squash channel.
  • WorldSquashTV. This is the official YouTube Channel of the World Squash Federation (WSF).
  • Polish Squash Association (PZSQ). The official Polish squash association channel.

Remember, if you know of other websites, channels or matches that should be added to this page, please contact me and I will add them - thank you.
