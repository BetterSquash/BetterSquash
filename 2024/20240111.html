5 Deadball Drills To Improve Your Accuracy

Hitting the ball non-stop is great, but sometimes simply dropping the ball and hitting it once can help us improve our accuracy. Let me show you 5 different drills to do just that.

11 January 2024 / 7-Min Read / Translate↗

Let's start with the obvious question: What is a deadball shot? A deadball shot is one where you drop and hit the the ball or throw and hit the ball. Not one where you hit a few in a row. A deadball shot is played one at a time. "But that doesn't recreate a real situation, Phillip" you say. And you are right. But plenty of things don't recreate real situations, but we still do them and they still bring benefits. Figure of eights come to mind.
