How Squash Rituals Can Help You

Having a custom that you follow before performing an important action can help reduce anxiety, focus your mind. In this article, I discuss and explain some rituals squash players perform.

01 January 2024 / 4-Min Read / Translate↗

Having a ritual that you follow before performing an important action can help reduce anxiety, focus your mind, and allow you to feel in control of a situation. Choosing the right custom or ritual is key. Possible one of the most obvious rituals in racket sports is Rafa Nadal's pre-serve ritual. I have written about why squash players touch the side wall before each point however, since the COVID pandemic and in the professional squash tour touching the wall has now been banned. At amateur level, I suppose many people still do it. Do you?

So in this article, I discuss and explain some other ritual squash players perform. Feel free to let me know the ones I have missed.
