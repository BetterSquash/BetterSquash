Video Chat

Coming Soon

Want to talk about your squash performance or your training? Do you have squash questions that you would like us to discuss?

I offer two options: 30 and 60 minutes. (Paid via PayPal)

You decide what we chat about, so please prepare some questions or talking points, and ideally send them to me beforehand, although that's not required.

I don’t need any videos of your playing - this is an opportunity for you to chat about squash, not an analysis of your playing.

This could be a perfect gift for a squash playing family-member, friend or colleague.

If you applied for the free video chat I offered previously, I will be in touch soon.

Depending on your location and my schedule, arranging a time suitable for both of us, may take a little time to organise.

When you are ready to start or if you have any questions, email me.

Cost: 30 Euros (30 minutes) and 50 Euros (60 minutes)(Paid via PayPal)
