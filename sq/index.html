Have Your Squash Questions Answered

This site has one purpose: to provide great answers for questions about squash. Questions could be about rules, training, coaching, tips, tactics, equipment, trivia or even history.

About This Website

There are plenty of websites, forums and social media accounts providing information about squash. But I felt a site dedicated to answering questions about squash in a straightforward, easy-to-read manner would benefit the squash community. Questions and answers are added two or three times per week.

To keep the website easy to use, each question and its answer has its own page. There's a search function on the complete Questions Archive. There's also an RSS/XML Feed available for those who like to stay updated without any effort.
