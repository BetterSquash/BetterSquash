Privacy & Cookie Policy

1. Who I Am

This website is operated by an individual squash enthusiast and coach. The aim is to provide helpful answers to squash-related questions and support players of all levels.

2. What Data Is Collected

This website does not collect any personal information directly. However, it uses Google Analytics to gather anonymous usage data. This helps me understand how people use the site so I can improve its content and structure.

3. Use of Cookies

Google Analytics uses cookies — small text files stored on your device — to collect standard internet log information and visitor behaviour data. This includes details like which pages are visited, how long visitors stay, and what devices or browsers they use. This data does not identify you personally.

4. Your Choices

You can choose whether to accept cookies when you first visit this site. If you decline, Google Analytics will not run. You can also manage or delete cookies via your browser settings at any time. If you’d prefer to block Google Analytics on all websites, you can install Google’s opt-out browser add-on.

5. Contact

If you have any questions about this policy, please contact privacy@squashquestions.com.
