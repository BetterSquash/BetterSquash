Squash Thought Of The Day

A more or less daily squash tip to help you improve and change the way you play squash.

December 2023

Spend at least 5 minutes every week hitting the ball as hard as you can. Don't worry about controlling it. You are doing it to get stronger. - 19 December 2023

Better to have a bad gameplan than no gameplan. At least with a bad one, you can see what doesn't work. You can also improve your tactical awareness. Lastly, by knowing what your plan is you commit to the shots, and sometimes if you play a less-than-optimum shot, but play it really well, it might even be a good shot. - 18 December 2023

.

One thing I love about squash, and I suppose pretty much all sports really, is that every time you walk onto court, it's a clean slate, a new day, a fresh start. No matter how cleanly you hit the ball or how well you moved the previous day, there's no guarantee you will do it as well today. Don't get complacent and assume yesterday's performance is a guide to today's. Walk onto court with as much determination to do you best as you always have. - 17 December 2023

.

You know how some people like to fall asleep to the sound of rain? Well, I love the sound of a squash ball hitting a squash court wall, especially on all glass courts. Maybe I should make a play list for Spotify. - 16 December 2023

.

Next time you play squash, try to hit the highest serve you can, even if it goes out. Once you hit a great serve, it becomes a little addictive. - 15 December 2023
