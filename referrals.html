Referral Programme Overview

When you sign up to any of my services, you receive a unique referral code. This is the code you give to the people you refer.

They MUST enter that code during purchase to receive a 10% discount on their purchase

You will receive increasing rewards as you refer more people.

As you would expect, there are some important conditions, but essentially the more people you refer who purchase one of my services, the more rewards you get.

Programme Details

To make sure there are no misunderstandings

How It Works: Individuals

1. Get Your Code: Receive a unique referral code when you sign up for any service (Video Analysis, Solo Routines, or Challenges).
2. Share With Others: Pass your code to fellow squash players through email, social media, or word of mouth.
3. They Save Money: When your referrals use your code at checkout, they receive a 10% discount on any service.
4. You Earn Rewards: We track all successful referrals linked to your code and notify you when you reach reward milestones.


Individual Reward Milestones

Referrals Your Reward
1 referral Your choice of a bonus Solo Routine or Challenge Group
3 referrals Your choice of a free Solo Routine Set or Challenge Set
6 referrals Free Video Analysis session
Every 3 additional referrals (9th, 12th, etc.) Another free Video Analysis session




Programme Terms

· Code Usage: Referral codes must be entered during checkout. Discounts cannot be applied retroactively.
· No Limits: You can refer unlimited people and share your code publicly on social media or forums.
· All Services Count: Referrals count toward your total regardless of which service is purchased.
· Repeat Purchases: Multiple purchases from the same person using your code all count as separate referrals.
· Commercial Licences: Referring someone who purchases a Commercial Licence counts as 2 referrals.
· Self-Referrals: You may use your own code once for a 10% discount and receive credit for 1 referral.
· No Expiration: Your referral achievements remain valid indefinitely.
· Processing Time: Please allow up to 48 hours after reaching a milestone to receive your confirmation email.
· Programme Updates: I reserve the right to modify these terms without advance notice to participants.




Commercial Licence Referrals

Commercial Licence referrals work differently to the Individual version. In effect, there is no referral rewards. However, the Referral Code can be added to the PDF sheets allow users to receive the 10% discount on my other services.

Frequently Asked Questions

Got some questions? Try here.

How do I get a referral code?
When you sign up for any of my services (Video Analysis, Solo Routines, or Challenges), you’ll automatically receive a unique referral code in your confirmation email.

How do I use my referral code?
Simply share your code with other squash players. When they enter it at checkout, they’ll receive a 10% discount, and the referral will be added to your total.

What rewards can I earn?
Your rewards increase as you refer more people:
1st referral: A bonus Solo Routine or a bonus Challenge Group – your choice.
3rd referral: A free Solo Routine Set or a free Challenge Set – your choice.
6th referral: A free Video Analysis.
Every 3 referrals after that: Another free Video Analysis (e.g., 9th, 12th, etc.).

Can I refer myself?
Yes! You’ll get the 10% discount, and it will count as **one** referral toward your total.

Can the same person use my referral code more than once?
Yes! If they purchase different services, each purchase counts as a separate referral.

What happens if someone forgets to enter my referral code at checkout?
Unfortunately, I can’t apply the code retroactively. The code **must** be entered during checkout.

Is there a time limit to collect referrals?
No, your referrals remain valid indefinitely. You can build up your total at your own pace.

Do all referrals count equally?
Yes, all service purchases count as one referral. However, if you refer a **Commercial Licence user**, it will count as **two referrals** toward your total.

How will I know when I’ve earned a reward?
I track all referrals manually. Once you reach a milestone, you’ll receive an email within **48 hours** with details on how to claim your reward.

How many people can I refer?
As many as you like! There is no limit to how many people can use your code.

Can I share my referral code publicly?
Yes! Feel free to post it on forums, social media, or share it with your squash community.

Can I change my referral code?
No, your code is unique and assigned to you permanently.

Can the terms of this programme change?
Yes, I reserve the right to update the referral programme if needed, especially if someone finds a clever loophole!

I have a question not listed here. How can I contact you?
Send an email to referrals@BetterSquash.com or join the BetterSquash Discord server and ask there.
